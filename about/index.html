About Me

Hello, this is Carlos Cuéllar, a UX Designer from Spain with an industrial design background and some love for all tech `things. When not staring at a screen, I'm either buying vinyl records or going to shows. I'm currently based in the Portland area, where I live with my two kids.

Now, the work stuff. I’ve been creating digital experiences that simplify user’s lives since 2006. I started working at marketing agencies in Spain, and after moving to the US in 2011 I became an in-house UX Designer specialized in B2B applications. I currently work at Trimble Inc. where we craft software solutions for construction companies.

If you need to contact me, please send an email to carlos@cuellar.fr

About This Site

This is the latest iteration of a personal website I started in the early 2000s. There are random notes about design, culture, and technology, and also links to resources I find interesting. In this new version, I’m also adding links to my music podcast.

You can check the source code at this Github repository. The site is built using Jekyll and it’s hosted in Netlify. The fonts used are DM Sans and DM Serif. If you want to follow the blog, you can subscribe to the RSS feed.

Both the content and the code created by me on this website are open source. Any other external content shared here is the property of their respective owners.
